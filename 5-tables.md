----------------

`\begin{across}{0.4}`{=latex}

: Grappling table

| Die  | Results            |
|:----:|:------------------:|
| 1--2 | Grappling succeeds |
| 3--6 | Attempt fails      |

the die roll. If both ships were motionless during the movement phase
subtract two from the die roll.
